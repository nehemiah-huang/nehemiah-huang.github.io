About

Structured in Thinking,

My work focuses on bringing clarity and structure to complex environments. I collaborate with founders and small teams where creativity, operations, and strategy must move together.

Background

From Systems to Strategy

My early training in accounting shaped how I approach problems — an instinct to look beneath the surface, understand how resources move, and see how structure influences outcomes.

I began my career in product support within a technology environment, working closely with product managers and engineers to support a large operational platform. That role exposed me to the mechanics of systems at scale. Over time, it became clear that what interested me most was not only the product itself, but the operational thinking behind it — the processes, workflows, and coordination that allow complex systems to function.

This realization led me to shift intentionally toward operational work. Since then, I have worked across a range of industries — from technology platforms to cultural publishing, creative studios, and experiential projects. My experience also extends across markets, including remote collaboration with teams in the United States and Ireland, supporting both sales and operational functions.

Today, my work often involves stepping into environments where ideas are moving quickly and helping bring order to the underlying systems. This may involve structuring workflows, coordinating collaborators, introducing financial visibility, or translating vision into executable steps.

Alongside this, my work is increasingly shaped by how AI can be used to improve operational clarity and speed. I integrate AI into workflows to reduce friction, surface insights faster, and support more efficient decision-making — keeping the structure behind it clear and intentional.

I am particularly drawn to environments where creativity and business intersect — cultural platforms, design studios, experiential projects, and founder-led ventures that require a careful balance between imagination and structure. Across all of this, curiosity remains central.

How I Typically Support

The Way We Work Together

The way we work together depends on what you are building and where you are in that journey. In some cases, you may need a short period of strategic clarity. In others, you may need someone more closely embedded in the day-to-day rhythm of the work.

01

Consultant

I step in to assess your systems, workflows, or ongoing projects and bring clarity to how things are structured. This often involves identifying operational gaps, refining how things are done, and helping you see where adjustments can create meaningful improvements.

02

Embedded Operator

I work within your business to help manage the operational side of it. This can involve coordinating projects, structuring workflows, and ensuring that ideas move forward with consistent execution.

03

Founder's Right Hand & Personal Assistant

The work becomes more integrated. I work directly alongside you to help carry the operational weight of the business so you can focus more fully on vision, relationships, and growth — managing priorities, following through on decisions, and handling the moving parts that would otherwise sit with you.
